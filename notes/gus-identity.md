# Gus Visual Identity

Gus is a fox-red Labrador retriever, about 40 pounds and compact for the breed. The pet should read as Gus first, not as a generic yellow lab.

## Core likeness

- Fox-red / copper coat with slightly darker ear and muzzle shading.
- Smooth short Labrador fur, no fluff, curls, spots, or white markings.
- Compact runt proportions: smaller, softer, slightly puppy-like body rather than tall or bulky.
- Rounded forehead and gentle brow creases above the eyes.
- Big dark glossy eyes, set wide enough to feel sweet and cuddly.
- Long soft floppy ears that hang beside the face, triangular with rounded ends.
- Medium-long Labrador muzzle, softened for chibi scale.
- Pink-brown liver nose with darker outline, not black.
- Sweet, trusting, cuddly expression; never goofy, derpy, aggressive, or hyper.
- Collar/harness can be simplified, but the base identity should not depend on accessories.

## Codex pet style

- Pixel-adjacent chibi mascot, readable at 192x208.
- Thick dark outline, flat cel shading, limited palette.
- Big head, compact body, tiny paws.
- Transparent-ready silhouette with no background, no text, no shadows, no detached effects.
- Keep Gus cute and emotionally warm, but not babyish to the point that he stops looking like a Labrador.

## Candidate evaluation

Choose the candidate that best balances:

- Gus-specific face and ears.
- Fox-red coat color.
- Cute Codex-pet readability at tiny size.
- Natural Labrador body language.
- No awkward, silly, or generic-dog feel.
